How can we help you?

Find answers to our most frequently asked questions about booking, cancellations, and travel logistics.

Categories

Booking

Booking is easy! Simply navigate to our Tours page, select your preferred itinerary, choose your travel dates and number of guests, and click 'Book Now'. Follow the checkout prompts to secure your spot.
Yes, we accommodate group bookings. For groups of 10 or more, please contact our group travel specialists through our Contact page or by emailing groups@nextstop.com for custom packages and discounts.

Cancellations

Yes, cancellations made up to 30 days prior to the tour start date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made between 14-29 days will receive a 50% refund, while cancellations made less than 14 days in advance are non-refundable.
In the rare event that we must cancel a tour due to weather, safety concerns, or low participation, you will be notified immediately and offered a full refund or the option to reschedule to another date or tour of equal value.

Payments

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Bank wire transfers are also accepted for group bookings and custom luxury itineraries.
Absolutely. All transactions are processed through a secure, PCI-compliant payment gateway with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.

Travel Info

Packing lists vary by destination and season. Once your booking is confirmed, we will email you a detailed pre-departure guide containing packing recommendations, weather insights, and travel tips specific to your tour.
We highly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance for all bookings. This helps cover unexpected medical expenses, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and other travel emergencies.
